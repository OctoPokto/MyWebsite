Anders Findal Jørgensen

I am a 27-year-old game designer from Norway. I like to make cool art and mess with technology. I am looking for work as a game designer or something fittingly adjacent. Currently, I am working on a prototype about growing plants. You can see some of that on the projects page. The goal is to polish my coding skills while making something cool.


I have always been fascinated by games. It was an escape from daily life at a young age. But it wasn't before entering high school that I realised I could actually make them too. Since that realisation, I couldn't shake the thought that this was the only thing I wanted to do for a living.
