Robotany

At its core, Robotany is a game idea about balancing an ecosystem with plants. This is my muse project. I come back to it with new ideas and experience once in a while and give it a shot, but so far it's just an idea, and ideas don't make money.

Laugh Track - GGJ 2024

Another hard theme with "Make me laugh". How do you make people laugh? You make a meta game where you are in an audience of a comedy show where you don't get the jokes, but have to laugh at the right parts unless you get eaten. Yeah, that was a challenge to make. And we didn't really nail it, but it was a really cool experience. I did the sound design again.

Play Laugh Track

Never Give Up - GGJ 2023

Ah, yes. My good good sweet boys, my bestest buddies. This year was truly fun. The theme was "Roots" and we bent the theme to fit "rooting for someone". So we made a game about cheering up depressed Japanese business men. I did the sound design on this one as will be a trend for the years to come.

Play Never Give Up

Why are you like this? - GGJ 2022

This year was a bit of a train wreck. "Duality" was the theme and i have no idea what I was going for with this one. There is a button and a text-to-speach voice is trying to convince you to not press the button. Can't have bangers every year, I learned how to communicate past my frustration with this one.

Tower Trouble - GGJ 2020

This years GGJ managed to sneak in right before Covid, phew! The theme was "Repair" and i had a neat little idea of moving up a tall tower, repairing it a bit before moving on. Slightly inspired by the GROW flash games of ye'olden times. I tried to do more programming this year. I remember quite a bit of hair getting pulled. Very happy with the outcome though.

Play Tower Trouble

Slice of Home - GGJ 2019

"What home means to you" is a passably vague theme. A bit hard when working in a team to find a thread that resonates with everyone. So I gathered some fellow students a year under me and we quickly landed on a short text based game where you are away from home for the first time. There are a few endings depending on your decitions. I made the music and helped keep the new students on track.

Play Slice of Home

The video, Game? - GGJ 2018

This was my first Global Game Jam game. It's not good. And that's okay. The theme for this year was "Transmission". The idea for my game was to tune a TV so you could see the transmission. You would turn two knobs to try and hit that sweet spot. This was absolutely the right level of complexity for one fo my first jams, but I still didn't have the right knowledge to make the game work.
