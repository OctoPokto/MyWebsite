Fruitbus New

I worked as an in-house Game Tester on Krillbite's game “Fruitbus”. In tandem with my testing work, I also made reports of community testing events, guided idle hands in testing at the office and community support after release.

Check out Fruitbus

Snufkin: Melody of Moominvalley

I worked on Snufkin as an in-house Game Tester pre-release. I took control over task management and got a feel for serious QA work. I also took a software tester certification to fill out my knowledge a bit.

Check out Snufkin

Mørkredd

Mørkredd was my first taste of game development outside a school setting. I wrote my bachelor's thesis while working on the game, writing about the fascinatingly vague topic of game feel. Hyper Games gave me possibly the best introductory experience into real-world game development I could ask for.

Check out Mørkredd

Jum Kin

Jum Kin is a small project where I try and replicate a game I have only watched a couple of videos of in Godot. It’s boring to make an exact replica, however, so I put a bit of my own spin on it. I have an incomplete version for testing on itch.

Check out Jum Kin

Robotany

At its core, Robotany is a game idea about balancing an ecosystem with plants. This is my muse project. I come back to it with new ideas and experience once in a while and give it a shot, but so far it's just an idea, and ideas don't make money.

Prototyping

Right now my prototyping is mostly focused on projects that help me develop my programming knowledge. I want to get better at making gameplay prototypes so I can display my ideas better. The goal is to put stuff out in the world instead of hoarding half finished projects.
